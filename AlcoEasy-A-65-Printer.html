AlcoEasy A-65: Professional Breath Analyzer for Accurate Alcohol Testing

The AlcoEasy A-65 Professional Breath Analyzer is India's premier alcohol testing device designed for law enforcement agencies, corporate safety programs, and industrial applications. With advanced electrochemical fuel cell sensor technology, this professional breathalyzer delivers laboratory-grade accuracy with instant printed results.

Our breath analyzer features a built-in Bluetooth thermal printer that provides immediate hard copies of test results - essential for legal documentation and compliance records. With storage for 50,000 test records and PC connectivity via USB, the A-65 simplifies data management and reporting requirements for organizations of all sizes.

The electrochemical fuel cell sensor in AlcoEasy A-65 is specifically engineered for breath alcohol testing. Unlike semiconductor sensors used in consumer devices, fuel cell technology provides superior accuracy, specificity to ethanol, and resistance to false positives from other substances. This makes it the preferred choice for professional applications where accuracy is critical.
